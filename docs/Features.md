# 🏥 KEY FEATURES OF AARUDHRA

---

1) Tamil text-to-speech (TTS) with NotoSansTamil font for audio-guided first-aid steps, accessible without reading skills.

2) Easy and quick first-aid guidance with step-by-step flows for cuts, burns, fever, stroke, snakebites, and headaches, aligned with guidance from WHO, ICMR, and IMA.

3) Easy access emergency buttons for quick actions and direct dialing of 101 and 108.

4) Uses OpenStreetMap to show nearby hospitals with distance and navigation support, designed to work even in low-network areas.

5) Tracks sugar, blood pressure, and heart-rate values and displays them as clear line charts for easy understanding. It also calculates and analyzes BMI.

6) Sends timely alerts to remind users to take their medicines regularly, even without internet.

7) Provides simple, conversational assistance in Tamil to help users access first-aid and health information easily.
